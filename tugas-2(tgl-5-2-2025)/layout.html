Bandung: Indonesia’s Creative Hub


Bandung, the capital of West Java, is known for its cool climate, colonial architecture, and thriving creative scene. It played a key role in Indonesia’s history, notably hosting the 1955 Asian-African Conference.


Top Attractions: Gedung Sate, Kawah Putih, Braga Street, and Tangkuban Perahu.


Culture & Innovation: Home to Sundanese traditions, ITB, and a booming fashion and tech industry.


Famous Foods: Batagor, seblak, and surabi.


Bandung’s mix of history, creativity, and natural beauty makes it a must-visit destination in Indonesia.
